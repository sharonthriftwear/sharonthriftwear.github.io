Our Story

Welcome to Sharon Thriftwear, your go-to destination for stylish and affordable ladies' fashion in Kiambu. Located at Ola Energy next to the stage, we're passionate about bringing you the latest trends in thriftwear without breaking the bank.

Our mission is simple: to provide every woman with access to fashionable clothing that makes them feel confident and beautiful. From maxi skirts to crop tops, we carefully curate our collection to ensure quality, style, and affordability.

How to Order

Simple steps to get your favorite items

Payment Method

All payments are made through Lipa Na M-Pesa. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Go to M-Pesa on your phone
  2. Select Lipa Na M-Pesa
  3. Enter Till Number 6918141
  4. Enter the amount for your order
  5. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and send
  6. Send the confirmation message to our WhatsApp (+254793022244)
