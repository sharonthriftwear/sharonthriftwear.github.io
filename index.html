Payments – M-Pesa Till

Till Number: 6918141

Use the steps below to pay securely for your order. Always confirm with us on WhatsApp before sending money.

  1. Go to M-Pesa on your phone.
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa" → "Buy Goods and Services".
  3. Enter Till Number: 6918141.
  4. Enter the amount agreed for your order.
  5. Confirm details then enter your M-Pesa PIN.
  6. Share the M-Pesa message screenshot on WhatsApp.

For cash payments at the physical shop, confirm with Sharon on arrival.
